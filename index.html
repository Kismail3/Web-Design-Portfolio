My Activism Project: Free and Accessible Education For All Children

A learning app/website for children could be a great solution to provide basic knowledge like math and english

About My Topic

The purpose of my website is to provide educational resources and tools for children, who do not have access to go to school, to learn basic skills such as math and literacy. Thus, creating a virtual classroom environment for remote or homeschooled children, promoting physical activity and movement in children, and offering a fun and engaging way for children to learn and explore new concepts.

Impact!

MIND

The problem of education not being free for all children can have a lasting effect on the mind, as it limits access to knowledge, stifles creativity, and hampers cognitive development.

SOCIETY

The problem of education not being free for all children can have a lasting effect on society, as it perpetuates inequality, limits social mobility, and hinders overall economic development.

HOME

Families and individuals might lack the financial stability to provide themselves or their children with an education. It might also lead to them thinking that gaining an education is not their right.
